# AI Inventory Loss & Wastage Predictor for Small Retailers

## Problem Statement
Small retail shop owners and MSMEs in India frequently face inventory losses due to overstocking, slow-moving items, expired goods, improper storage practices, and incorrect demand estimationâ€”especially for grains, pulses, and perishable goods during seasonal and climatic changes. Although sales data exists through billing systems or manual records, it is rarely analyzed to prevent losses proactively.

## Proposed Solution
This project proposes an AI-powered inventory risk prediction system that analyzes historical sales data to identify products at high risk of wastage or spoilage. In addition to risk identification, the system provides AI-guided storage and handling recommendations to help retailers take preventive actions and reduce losses.

## Target Users
- Kirana store owners  
- Small retail businesses  
- Local MSMEs  

## Key Features
- Upload or use existing sales data (CSV format)
- Identify slow-moving and dead-stock items
- Predict inventory items at risk of expiry or spoilage
- AI-guided storage and handling recommendations for high-risk inventory items (e.g., grains, pulses, packaged goods)
- Festival and season-aware demand insights
- Simple text-based explanations instead of complex dashboards

## How AI is Used
A Large Language Model (LLM) analyzes structured sales data along with contextual factors such as product type, seasonality, and sales velocity. Based on identified risk patterns, the AI generates human-readable insights explaining:
- Why certain items are at risk
- What preventive storage or handling actions can reduce losses

## Expected Impact
- Reduced inventory wastage and spoilage
- Improved storage practices for grains and packaged goods
- Better purchasing and stocking decisions
- Increased profitability for small retailers
- Adoption of practical, data-driven decision-making among MSMEs
